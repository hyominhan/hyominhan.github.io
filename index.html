Hyomin Han

Hi! I am a Master's student in the Learning Sciences program atNorthwestern University, where I explore the intersection of creative learning, artificial intelligence, and tangible interaction design.

My research sits at the nexus of three areas: tangible interfaces that support creativity, AI systems that preserve human agency, and inclusive participation in collaborative experiences. I investigate how understanding human learning and machine cognition can guide the design of technologies that augment—rather than replace—human creativity. I apply these ideas primarily in music education. I build systems that combine the adaptability of AI with the intuitive nature of physical interfaces, creating personalized learning experiences that are accessible, engaging, and meaningful for learners of all backgrounds.
